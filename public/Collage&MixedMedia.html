Materials Used

Each piece is created using archival-quality papers, fabrics, and adhesives to ensure longevity. Materials may include vintage papers, textiles, found objects, acrylics, and other mixed media elements carefully selected for their visual and textural qualities.

Protection & Care

Collage works are sealed with a protective varnish to preserve layers and prevent deterioration. Display away from direct sunlight and high humidity. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. Do not use water or cleaning solutions on the surface.

Framing & Display

Most pieces come mounted on sturdy backing and are ready to frame. We recommend professional framing with UV-protective glass for optimal preservation. For three-dimensional assemblages, shadow box framing is recommended.

Shipping & Packaging

Collage artworks are carefully packaged with protective layers, rigid backing boards, and secure wrapping. Shipped via insured courier with tracking. Delivery typically takes 5-8 business days. Each piece arrives with a certificate of authenticity.
