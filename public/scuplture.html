White Glove Delivery

All sculptures are shipped via specialized art couriers with white glove service. This includes custom crating, climate-controlled transportation, delivery to room of choice, unpacking, and debris removal. Delivery typically takes 3-4 weeks.

Insurance & Protection

Your sculpture is fully insured during transit. We use museum-quality packing materials and methods to ensure safe arrival. Each piece is inspected before and after shipping with photographic documentation.

Installation Support

For larger sculptures, we provide installation guidance and can coordinate with local art handlers for proper placement and mounting. Detailed care instructions are included with every purchase.

Authenticity & Provenance

Each sculpture includes a certificate of authenticity signed by the artist, detailing the work's provenance, materials, edition number (if applicable), and care recommendations. Keep this documentation with the artwork.
