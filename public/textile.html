Each textile artwork is carefully wrapped in acid-free tissue paper and shipped in a sturdy tube or flat box to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, with express options available.

Every piece comes with a signed certificate of authenticity from the artist, detailing the materials used, techniques employed, and care instructions. This adds value and provenance to your artwork.

Care Instructions

Keep textile art away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. Dust gently with a soft brush. For cleaning, consult a professional textile conservator. Store flat or rolled (never folded) in acid-free materials if not displayed.
