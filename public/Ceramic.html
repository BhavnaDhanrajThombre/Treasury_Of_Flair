Cleaning & Maintenance

Most pieces are dishwasher safe, but hand washing is recommended to preserve the glaze's beauty. Use mild soap and warm water. Avoid abrasive scrubbers that might scratch the surface.

Temperature Care

Microwave and oven safe up to 220°C unless otherwise noted. Avoid sudden temperature changes to prevent thermal shock. Let pieces cool gradually before washing.

Storage Tips

Store pieces with padding between them to prevent chipping. Keep in a dry environment away from extreme temperature fluctuations. Stack bowls and plates carefully with protective layers.

Shipping & Handling

Each piece is carefully wrapped and packaged with bubble wrap and protective materials. Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Tracking information provided upon dispatch.
