Care Instructions

Display away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. Avoid hanging in areas with high humidity. Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth. For acrylic paintings, you may use a slightly damp cloth if needed. Do not use cleaning chemicals or water on oil paintings.
